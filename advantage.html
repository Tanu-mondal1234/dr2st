Rise of Digital Payment

The Indian payment system has come a long way in the last few decades.There was a time when people fought over the acceptance of torn notes taped together. However, even the remotest areas today have hybrid payment options ranging from cash, Debit or Credit Card, UPI, NetBanking, Mobile Banking, and more.

Advantages:--

Less Complexity

The options of Digital Payments can be accessed anytime and anywhere, removing the need to visit banks or ATMs often.

Faster payments

Digital payment options like UPI, Debit Cards, etc., are quick to use. They take a few seconds, even for large sums of money, and can be accessed anytime during the day.

Safer transactions

There is a transparent trail of transactions that helps minimize fraud. Everything is done digitally, removing the chances of error or human mistakes.
